Key Themes

  • Surreal Humor: The show takes normal, mundane activities like playing video games or skipping work and turns them into absurd, cosmic adventures.
  • Nostalgia: "Regular Show" frequently references 1980s and 1990s pop culture, including old-school video games, music, and movies.
  • Friendship: At its core, the show is about the bond between Mordecai and Rigby, their ups and downs, and how they stick together through even the most bizarre situations.
  • Responsibility vs. Fun: Mordecai and Rigby constantly struggle between having fun and taking responsibility for their actions, a key conflict throughout the show.
  • Growth: The characters, particularly Mordecai and Rigby, mature as the series progresses, dealing with relationships, personal growth, and eventually moving on to more adult roles.
